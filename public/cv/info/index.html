About

Since 2024, I am a post-doc at the Catholic University of Louvain in Louvain-la-Neuve (Belgium), where I work on injective deformation of meshes in the context of the X-mesh project, lead by Jean-François Remacle and Nicolas Moës.

I obtained a PhD from the University of Lorraine under the supervision of Etienne Corman and Dmitry Sokolov. I defended my PhD in December 2023.

Research Interests

  • Geometry Processing
  • Meshing
  • Mesh parametrization
  • Deep learning and neural implicit representations
